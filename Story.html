☕ Our Story:

 It all started with a simple idea — to create a space where coffee isn’t just a
    
drink, but an experience. A place where people could slow down, connect, and feel

at home.

Founded in [2025] by [Pitambar Tiwari], our little coffee shop began as a 

neighborhood dream. Nestled in the heart of [NAGPUR], we opened our doors with 

nothing more than a second-hand espresso machine, a few cozy chairs, and a

whole lot of heart.

We believed in sourcing coffee ethically, roasting it with care, and serving

every cup with purpose. Our beans are handpicked from small, sustainable farms

around the world, then locally roasted to bring out their unique flavor and

character.

But our story isn't just about coffee. It's about people — the early risers 

grabbing a quiet moment before the day begins, the friends catching up over 

lattes, the artists, students, dreamers, and thinkers who make our space 

feel alive.

At [ENDLESS COFFEE SHOP], you're not just a customer. You're part of the story 

we’re writing every day — one sip, one smile, one conversation at a time.

Whether you’re here for the coffee, the community, or just a quiet corner 

to read, welcome. We saved you a seat.

— With love,
The [ENDLESS COFFEE SHOP] Team
